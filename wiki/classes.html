Classes & Spells

Pick a vocation on the title screen (switch anytime with P). The controls bar only shows the commands for your current class.

Mage ★★★★★

Weave eight raw elements into anything. Queue up to five with QWER / ASDF, then release with RMB (aimed), Shift+RMB (self), or C (area burst). The staff orb glows with your current selection.

Elements

KeyElementNotes
QWaterWets targets — sets up freeze and lightning combos
WLifeHeals — but sears the undead
EShieldWards yourself or raises walls
RColdChills; freezes wet targets solid
ALightningArcs between enemies; doubled vs wet
SArcaneRaw beam damage
DEarthBoulders; 3× shatter vs frozen
FFireIgnites (unless wet); burn ticks over time

Chemistry

  • Fusions: Water+Fire → Steam · Water+Cold → Ice · Steam+Cold → Water · Ice+Fire → Water
  • Opposites cancel in the queue: Fire↔Cold · Water↔Lightning · Earth↔Lightning · Life↔Arcane · Shield↔Shield
  • Duplicates multiply a spell's punch ×1.7 each.
  • Shape priority: Shield → wall · Earth → boulder · Ice → shard volley · Arcane/Life → beam · fluids → spray · Lightning → arc.

Alchemist ★★★★☆

Eight finished potions on cooldowns — no mixing, no wand, just a satchel and good aim. Throw with RMB or drink with Shift+RMB. Flowers bloom where you walk.

KeyPotionEffect
QMushroomPoison zone; poison spreads between enemies
WCatnipSummons distracting cats
EPortalTwo portals link — anything touching one exits the other
RHealRestoring splash (undead hate it)
AMoonshineConfusion — reversed controls
SButterfingersSlick zone; enemies drop their weapons
DHasteSpeed boost
FRainA personal raincloud — wets everything under it
Classic opener, either class: get the target wet (Water spray or Rain), then follow with Lightning (double damage) or Cold (frozen solid) and finish with Earth for the 3× shatter.

Co-op

Press O for the co-op panel. One player clicks Host and sends the generated code to a friend over any chat; the friend clicks Join, pastes it, accepts, and sends back the reply code; the host accepts that. Both wizards — names, classes, and all — now share the host's world: the host's monsters are everyone's monsters, and travel follows the host. Experimental, two players, no server involved.
