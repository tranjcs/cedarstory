Respawn rules

Monsters refill in waves every 10 minutes per stage, up to the stage's cap and never right on top of you. At night the spawn clock runs twice as fast — see Day & Night.

Resistances multiply incoming damage: 0 = immune, 0.4 = takes 40%, 1.5 = takes half again as much. Undead creatures are damaged by healing magic.
