Welcome to CedarStory

CedarStory is a top-down arcane sandbox: queue elements on the keyboard, chain them into compound spells, and explore a world of fifteen connected stages — from the monster-dense Wilds to far ports across the Sapphire Crossing. It runs in the browser with no install: play it here.

Getting started

New wizard? Pick a name, choose the Mage or Alchemist, whack the training dummies, then follow the worn road north to Willowbrook.

The short version of everything

  • Fifteen stages — wilds, farms, two towns, a keep and its dungeon, a goblin camp, a giant ocean, an island, a haunted pirate ship, and four far ports. See The World.
  • Day & night — one in-game day passes every 20 real minutes. Shops close at dusk, lamps light, townsfolk sleep, and monsters spawn twice as fast. See Day & Night.
  • Monsters respawn in waves every 10 minutes per stage. See the Bestiary.
  • Townsfolk live their own lives — they chat with each other, keep pets, and go home at night. One of them needs your help. See Townsfolk.
  • Two-player co-op — press O, host or join with a copy-paste code, and a second named wizard drops into your world. Experimental.

Spell chemistry crash course

  • Combine: Water+Fire → Steam · Water+Cold → Ice · Steam+Cold → Water · Ice+Fire → Water
  • Opposites annihilate: Fire↔Cold · Water↔Lightning · Earth↔Lightning · Life↔Arcane · Shield↔Shield
  • Statuses: wet doubles lightning · wet+cold freezes · frozen shatters under earth or ice · healing sears the undead · poison spreads between enemies
